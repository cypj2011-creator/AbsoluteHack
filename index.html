About Us

Our Mission

To inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and develop the next generation of STEM leaders through competitive robotics excellence.

Our Vision

To be a leading FTC team that consistently pushes the boundaries of engineering design, programming innovation, and team collaboration while maintaining the highest standards of gracious professionalism.

Founded with a passion for robotics and a commitment to excellence, Team 28028 Absolute Hack was born from the belief that through dedication, innovation, and teamwork, we can achieve the absolute best in everything we do.

Our Evolution

1st Season - The Beginning

2021-2022

Our journey began with nine dedicated founding members: Calvin, Xin Ru, Kai Chuen, Kai Jie, Yee Sheng, Zi Zheng, Jing Song, Jun Sheng. Starting from scratch, we learned the fundamentals of FTC robotics, built our first robot, and established the foundation of Team Absolute Hack.

Team Formation First Robot Build Competition Debut 2021/2022 Robot Malaysia Championship
